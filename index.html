About us

Saul Goodman & Associates is a law firm with its office in Boulder, CO. The wealth of practice areas and experience, as well as the educational background and competence of Saul Goodman, make Saul Goodman & Associates a unique legal service provider for struggling individuals, convicts, and criminals.

The firm's practice encompasses a wide variety of criminal defense laws, with a special focus on federal and state criminal defense, misdemeanor crimes, and criminal appeals processes. Saul Goodman has an extensive network of connections through which he acquires necessary resources to successfully win cases. He also counsels struggling convicts, ex-convicts, and full-fledged criminals on how to effectively evade the law, helping them to exploit various loopholes.

As a former almost-criminal himself, Saul Goodman is a highly skilled attorney that once experienced first-hand the problems that criminals and convicts encounter daily. Although Boulder is known for its low crime rates, the location of Saul Goodman's office is strategic, diverting unneeded attention away from his clients.
